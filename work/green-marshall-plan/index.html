A Green Marshall Plan

I created this report as part of a five-person group during Fall 2019 in the Climate Change Solutions class at UCSB. In addition to designing and typesetting the report in LaTeX, I authored Part 3: "A Green Marshall Plan". In it, I outline a series of policy proposals to reduce emissions and promote US climate leadership on the world stage through an ambitious foreign aid and green energy research plan.
